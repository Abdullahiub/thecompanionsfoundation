Project and Maintenance Committee

Alh. Abdulkadir Yelwa Idris
Chairman

Responsibilities of the Project and Maintenance Committee

  • Maintain the Foundation mosques and schools in terms of remodeling, repairs, water and electricity supplies or any other infrastructural facility that would be required in the mosques or the schools.
  • Initiate and monitor construction of drains and cleaning of gutters around the environment.
  • Maintain graveyard located within the environment.
  • Conceive the idea of any project the community deemed beneficial and bring to the attention of the Board for necessary action.
  • Supervise and manage any project embarked upon by the Foundation.
