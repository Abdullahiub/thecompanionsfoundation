Responsibilities of the Board of Trustees

  • Be the administrative organ of the Foundation.
  • Formulate policies and programmes, and observe implementation of same for the attainment of the Foundation aims and objectives.
  • Acquire and dispose of properties on behalf of the Foundation in consultation with the members.
  • Safeguard the properties, protect and promote the ideals of the Foundation.
  • Appoint members of statutory committees of the Foundation.
  • Consider and approve annual budgets of Committees of the Foundation.
  • Appoint external auditors for auditing of the Foundation accounts.
  • Engage, employ or contract with any person whom it considers fit to help in the attainment of the Foundation aims and objectives.
  • Disengage the services of any person whom it considers unfit to help in the attainment of the Foundation aims and objectives.
  • Present periodic report of activities to members.
  • Review of the Foundation Constitution when the need arises.
  • Consider and treat accordingly any issue that fall into the Board purview.
